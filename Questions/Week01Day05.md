**Assignments:**

Given a value N, find out the largest palindrome number which is the product of two N-digit numbers.

Examples:

Input: N = 2
Output: 9009
Explanation:
9009 is the largest number which is product of two 2-digit numbers 91 and 99 (9009 = 91*99)
